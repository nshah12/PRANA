S3 within a single region already replicates across 3 AZs — giving 99.999999999% (11 nines) durability. A second region adds disaster recovery, not just availability. True 2× active-active for blobs doubles your storage bill without doubling revenue. The strategies below let you stay resilient at 1.33×–1.5× cost.
