PRANA Mobile — Full Prototype, New Visual Direction (16 screens)

New directionAll 16 screens — onboarding through settings
Breaking from the web-portal layout toward a mobile-native identity: animated illustration on launch, a deep space/journey palette with an indigo→cyan→emerald accent gradient, glassmorphism, and bigger, more confident type (Space Grotesk + Inter). The splash tells PRANA's actual story — a career thread across employers, converging into one vault that's permanently yours.

Provenance icons (screen 3) replace the "VERIFIED" text badge — a green shield for employer-pushed documents, an amber upload arrow for self-uploads, and an indigo checkmark reserved for future third-party-verified sources (DigiLocker etc.), per document.source_type from §29. No legend bar — the icons read on their own, and document cards are now a soft light-gray (#F0EFEA) so they sit distinctly below the white stat cards.

Full set: 1 Splash · 2 Sign in · 3 Set up 2FA (conditional) · 4 Enter code · 5 Register device · 6 Enable Face ID · 7 Biometric unlock (returning user) · 8 Push-approval login · 9 Vault home · 10 Menu · 11 Activity · 12 Career · 13 Self-upload + consent (§29) · 14 Create share · 15 Settings · 16 Document viewer.

Dark "space/journey" screens (1–8, 16) carry the gradient + glassmorphism language; light screens (9–15) use the warm off-white surface with gray (#F0EFEA) cards, the floating glass pill nav, and provenance icons throughout.

Screens 17–18 address two user-delight gaps benchmarked against Revolut/Apple Wallet/PhonePe: 17 shows the proactive "new document" banner (gradient, dismissible) plus a numbered bell badge — so an employer push is felt, not just discoverable by scrolling; 18 is the empty state a brand-new employee sees before their first document arrives, with an on-brand illustration, a progress checklist, and a self-upload fallback. Motion, haptics, and loading-state specs are documented separately in PRANA_Mobile_DesignLanguage.md.
