01
Platform Overview
What PRANA is and the core architectural premise
▼
All Roles

PRANA (Personal Repository And Networked Authentications) is a multitenant SaaS platform that gives every employee a single, permanent, portable document identity — one URL that persists across employers, survives job changes, and accumulates documents from every organisation that participates in the programme.

The central premise: the employee is the owner and custodian of their professional documents. Employers are tenants — they push documents into a vault during and for a configured window after the employment relationship. The vault belongs to the employee and never expires.

One Employee. One URL. Forever. prana.in/vault/rahul-sharma-xk72 resolves to Rahul's vault whether he is currently at Company A, an alumnus of Company B, or between jobs. It never changes. It never transfers to an employer.

The post-exit continuity model: on employee exit, the employer's push rights continue for a configurable window X (default 6 months, range 3–12 months, configurable per grade/band). After the window closes, employer write access is auto-revoked. Employee read access is permanent and unconditional.
